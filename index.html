Fastest Voice Streaming

Claude consistently delivers first tokens within 100–200ms with smooth, predictable streaming — the critical requirement for a voice agent that can't feel robotic or lag mid-sentence.

~150ms first-token latency · vs GPT-4o ~200ms in dialog

200K Token Context

Real-Time Call Flow

What Happens on Every Call

From the moment a lead picks up to the CRM update — the complete signal path through the ATOM stack, in sequence.

ATOM Lead Gen / Campaign Call
Full-stack signal path · ~100–200ms total response latency
01
Twilio SIP Trunk — Call Initiated
twilio.com · PSTN / WebRTC · Outbound or Inbound
Twilio routes the call via SIP directly to the Hume EVI endpoint on SambaCloud. No Akamai Linode proxy — the voice bridge bottleneck is eliminated. For ATOM Campaign, the auto-dialer initiates the outbound call with AMD (answering machine detection) pre-filter.
02
Hume EVI 4 — Prosody & Emotion Capture
SambaCloud · EVI 4 · Real-time prosody analysis
As the lead speaks, EVI simultaneously transcribes speech AND extracts emotional vectors — tone, rhythm, timbre, cadence. Happiness, anxiety, determination, hesitation are all detected in real-time without waiting for an STT transcript to complete.
03
Vocal End-of-Turn Signal Detected
EVI vocal signal detection · ≈0ms interruption error
EVI detects natural speech completion via vocal cues — not a silence timer. This prevents the signature flaw of traditional voice AI: interrupting mid-thought. ATOM waits for the lead to actually finish speaking before responding.
04
Claude Sonnet/Opus — Response Generation
Anthropic via Hume EVI · 200K context · ~100–200ms first token
The eLLM feeds transcript + emotional context to Claude. Claude accesses the full conversation history (200K tokens), company RAG data, lead profile, and objection scripts — generating a response calibrated to both the content and emotional state of the caller. First tokens arrive in ~100–200ms.
05
Octave TTS — Emotionally Modulated Speech
Hume Octave v2 · Expressive synthesis · Streaming output
Claude's response streams directly into Octave TTS, which generates speech with prosody, pacing, and emotional register matched to the response intent. ATOM doesn't sound robotic — it sounds like a confident, empathetic human agent.
06
ATOM Lead Gen Logic — Qualification & Routing
ATOM Core · Nirmata Holdings · Real-time decision engine
In parallel with conversation, ATOM's lead gen engine scores the prospect, tracks qualification milestones, handles objections with pre-calibrated response trees, and decides whether to push to booking, nurture, or disengage — all within the natural flow of conversation.
07
GPT-4o — Tool Calls & CRM Writes
OpenAI · Async structured output · Background execution
While Claude handles conversation, GPT-4o executes async tool calls: writing to Salesforce/HubSpot, booking demo calendar slots, triggering follow-up sequences, and logging structured call summaries. Isolated from the voice loop so tool execution never adds latency to ATOM's responses.
08
Post-Call — Sync, Score & Schedule
CRM · Pinecone Vector DB · ATOM Campaign Queue
On call end: full transcript + emotion timeline logged, lead score updated, disposition tagged (qualified/nurture/DNC), follow-up task created, and call recording linked. ATOM Campaign queues the next action automatically based on call outcome.

The 5 Acoustic Pillars of a Viral Voice

01

Fundamental Frequency (F0)

Speakers with a low fundamental vocal frequency are perceived as more dominant and authoritative. Leaders who also vary their F0 dramatically — swooping from low to high — are perceived as charismatic.

02

Pitch Range

A wide melodic pitch range signals passion and engagement. Studies on Steve Jobs found his pitch range was exceptionally large — moving far beyond the typical male speaker band — the highest of any studied business speaker.

Record High

03

Loudness Dynamics

The ability to dramatically whisper then roar — varying loudness within a speech — creates emotional peaks that lock listener attention. Jobs' dynamics waxed and waned extensively throughout every keynote.

Dynamic Range (whisper → roar)
Wide — Waxes & Wanes Extensively

04

Tempo & Fluency

Speaking in short, punchy bursts with strategic pauses creates anticipation. Jobs' speech rate was faster than average, yet felt fluent because his pauses were perfectly placed — creating a signature theatrical intimacy.

Vowel Space

Clear, full vowel articulation (large acoustic vowel space) signals intelligence and conviction. Research comparing Jobs to Zuckerberg found Jobs' vowel space significantly larger — a key measurable marker of charismatic delivery.
